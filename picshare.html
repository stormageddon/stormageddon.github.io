PicShare for Mac

PicShare is a Mac app that allows you to easily take and share screen shots. As a minimalistic menubar application, PicShare stays out of your way while giving you the ability to instantly share your screen captures through any medium you want. PicShare was inspired by my need to always share really quick snapshots with friends via a url, but not wanting to have to deal with the overhead of any sort of image management. As such, PicShare only allows you to access your last 10 images - everything else is automatically cleaned up. The point is to have a quick, disposable link to your image!

The PicShare client is also open source! If you want to take a look at it yourself, or you want to file a bug or feature request, you can find it on GitHub PicShare is currently in fairly early stages of development, so you can always get the latest dev build and run that as well. The backend is built on CloudMine, guaranteeing secure storage of your user information and photos.
